Careers 
Registered Nurse
  A Registered Nurse (RN) is a healthcare professional who has completed the necessary training and education to have a nursing license. They are responsible for providing and coordinating patient care,educating patients and families about health issues and collaborating with other healthcare professionals.
Certified Nursing Assistant
Certified Nursing Assistants(CNAs) offer essential patient care under nurse supervision, commonly in nursing homes. They aid with daily tasks like bathing,dressing,feeding,and mobility. Additionally, CNAs monitor vital signs, and report any changes to nursing staff. 
Nurse Practitioner
 A Nurse Practitioner (NPs) is a highly trained nurse who can diagnose illnesses, prescribe medicine, and oversee patient care, much like doctors. They specialize in areas like family health or mental health and need extra education and certification.
State Tested Nursing Assistant
 A State Tested Nursing Assistant (STNA) is a trained caregiver who helps patients with basic needs such as bathing, feeding, and moving around. They work under the supervision of nurses in places like nursing homes or hospitals.STNAs play a vital role in ensuring comfort.
