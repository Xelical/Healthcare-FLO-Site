About 
Vital Signs 
Get hands on experience feeling for and recording rate of pulse, taking respirations, taking blood pressure, and locating an artery. You will learn how to use the basic tools necessary for everyday hospital procedures.   
Hygiene 
Making sure the resident is clean and confident is our most important goal. Proper hygiene consists of mouth care (brushing teeth), hair care, nail care, showering, and partial bed baths.     
Wheelchair 
Familiarize yourself with transferring patients from their bed via a wheelchair. Minimize the risk of falls or injuries by positioning patients correctly, learning how to maintain balance, esnsure smooth transitions, and avoid obstacles.    
PPE 

Prevent infections by using the necessary personal protective equipment: gowns, masks, goggles, gloves, etc.  

Feeding 
Learn how to form meals that are coordinated with diet cards. Get experience telling patients what's on their plate, feeding them, and cleaning up, practicing proper hygiene throughout.
